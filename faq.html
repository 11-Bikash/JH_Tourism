Why is Jharkhand famous for tourism?

Located in the eastern part of India, Jharkhand is blessed with lush green forests, lofty hills, sparkling waterfalls, several wildlife sanctuaries, and a treasure trove of mineral reserves.

How many tourists visit Jharkhand?

A total of 104.19 lakh visits by domestic tourists and same day visitors were made in Jharkhand at the 59 important tourist destinations identified by the State Tourism Department.

Which place of Jharkhand will be developed as tourist zone?

There are famous tourist destinations like Palamu Tiger Reserve, Belta Fort. Tourists from different parts of the world visit Palamu Reserve Forest.

Which is the most beautiful City in Jharkhand?

Ranchi. Known as the "City of Waterfalls”, Ranchi is the capital of Jharkhand and one of the most tourist spots in this area.

What language is spoken in Jharkhand?

It has been observed that the main language that is spoken throughout Jharkhand is Khortha. This language is also known as the eastern Magahi language. Apart from this several other languages such as Magahi and Bhojpuri are also spoken in different parts of Jharkhand.

Which city is called Queen of Jharkhand?

Rani(queen) channamma was called by another famous name by 'keladi channamma'. Netarhat is a town in Latehar district in the Indian state of Jharkhand. Referred to as the “Queen of Chotanagpur”, it is a popular hill station.

What is Jharkhand national dish?

Pittha is the famous dish of Jharkhand having origin from West Bengal. It is usually stuffed with aloo ka chokha, different varieties of boiled dals, khoa, or thickened milk.
